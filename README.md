<p align="left">Welcome to our Robotics Research Lab repository. This laboratory is part of the Federal University of Santa Catarina (UFSC), Blumenau Campus.<br><br>This space brings together projects, experiments, publications, and tools developed through our research activities in areas such as mobile robotics (ground and aerial), autonomous systems, and artificial intelligence. Our goal is to promote open, reproducible, and collaborative research, enabling students, researchers, and developers to explore, reuse, and contribute to our work.<br><br>We are open to collaborations involving the development of custom solutions and services in robotics and intelligent systems for academic and industry partners.</p>
